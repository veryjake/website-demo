Favorite Summer Activities!

During the summer, I really enjoy going to lakes. My favorite location is Red Fish Lake up by Stanley, Idaho. I love going there with my family to camp, boat, or fish. One of my other favorite summer activities is going to get ice cream with friends, I love the contrast between the hot weather and a cold ice cream! Lastly, I enjoy reading a good book in the summer evenings, when the weather is just right before the sun goes all the way down. Super fun and enjoyable, definitely would recommend!
